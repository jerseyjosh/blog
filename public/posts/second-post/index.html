Modern Web Development in 2026#

The web development landscape continues to evolve at a rapid pace. Here are my thoughts on where we’re heading.

Static Site Generators Are Here to Stay#

Static sites offer incredible benefits:

  • Performance - No server-side processing means faster load times
  • Security - No database means fewer attack vectors
  • Cost - Host for free on platforms like GitHub Pages, Netlify, or Vercel
  • Simplicity - Just HTML, CSS, and JavaScript

The Terminal Aesthetic#

I’m really digging this retro terminal look. There’s something nostalgic about monospace fonts and simple layouts that just works.

Typography Matters#

The choice of Fira Code as the default font really makes a difference. Ligatures and proper character spacing enhance readability significantly.

Building in Public#

One thing I’m trying this year is building and learning in public. Sharing progress, even small wins, helps with:

  • Accountability
  • Getting feedback early
  • Connecting with like-minded people
  • Creating a portfolio of work

Random Thoughts#

Sometimes the best ideas come when you’re not actively trying to solve a problem. Taking breaks, going for walks, and letting your mind wander can lead to creative breakthroughs.

“The only way to do great work is to love what you do.” - Steve Jobs
